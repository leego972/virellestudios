## TIMELINE OVERVIEW
The pilot takes place over approximately 24 hours, from 4:30 AM to 3:00 AM the next day. There are three distinct wardrobe blocks for the adults:
1.  **Early Morning / Sleepwear** (Scenes 1-2)
2.  **Daywear / Workwear** (Scenes 3-5)
3.  **Evening / The Dinner** (Scenes 6-14)
