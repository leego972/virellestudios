# Finalized Linked-Family & Twin Resemblance Map

The Virelle Signature Cast is unique in its ability to provide pre-linked, mathematically consistent family units. This system solves one of the hardest problems in AI generation: maintaining believable familial resemblance without creating identical clones.

Below is the finalized mapping for all linked units in the 24-actor roster.

## 1. The Vance Family (Father/Son or Brothers)
**Linked Actors:** Julian Vance (M-L01) & Sammy Vance (B-Y01)

- **Shared Facial Anchors:** Inter-pupillary distance, left-leaning asymmetrical smile, heavy-lidded eye shape, olive skin undertone.
- **Divergence Anchors:** Sammy has softer jawline architecture, a larger iris-to-sclera ratio (youth marker), and lacks Julian's 3mm stubble anchor.
- **Shared Body-Family Logic:** Both possess a lean, narrow-shouldered frame that reads as elegant rather than physically imposing.
- **Do-Not-Drift Rules:** Sammy's eye color and hair texture must remain mathematically locked to Julian's base parameters. If a user modifies Julian's hair color in a project, Sammy's must automatically update to match.
