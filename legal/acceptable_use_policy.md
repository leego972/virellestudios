# Acceptable Use Policy for Virelle Studios

**Last Updated: March 5, 2026**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules and guidelines for using the Virelle Studios Services. Violation of this AUP may result in suspension or termination of your account.

## Prohibited Content and Activities

You may not use the Services to create, upload, or distribute any of the following:

- **Illegal Content:** Content that is illegal under applicable law.
- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** We have a zero-tolerance policy for CSAM. Any such content will be reported to law enforcement.
- **Hate Speech:** Content that promotes violence or incites hatred against individuals or groups based on race or ethnic origin, religion, disability, age, nationality, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, or any other characteristic that is associated with systemic discrimination or marginalization.
- **Harassment:** Content that is abusive, harassing, or threatening to others.
- **Non-Consensual Explicit Content:** Content that depicts sexual acts or nudity of individuals without their explicit consent.
- **Copyright Infringement:** Content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others.

## Prohibited Technical Misuse

You may not:

- Use any automated system to access the Services in a manner that sends more request messages to the Virelle servers than a human can reasonably produce in the same period.
- Attempt to interfere with, compromise the system integrity or security of, or decipher any transmissions to or from the servers running the Service.
- Take any action that imposes, or may impose at our sole discretion, an unreasonable or disproportionately large load on our infrastructure.

We reserve the right to determine, in our sole discretion, what constitutes a violation of this AUP.
