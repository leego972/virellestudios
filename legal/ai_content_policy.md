When you generate content that includes the likeness of a real person, you are responsible for obtaining the necessary permissions.

By using our Services, you agree to indemnify Virelle for any claims arising from your use of a person's likeness without proper authorization.

### Copyright

The copyright status of AI-generated content is a complex and evolving area of law. You are responsible for how you use the content you create and for ensuring that your use does not violate copyright law.

In addition to the restrictions in our Acceptable Use Policy, you may not use our AI tools to knowingly create or promote:

We are committed to the ethical use of AI and will take action on accounts that violate this policy.
