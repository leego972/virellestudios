# Privacy Policy for Virelle Studios

**Last Updated: July 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Virelle, Inc. ("Virelle", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Services. 

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, as well as information that is automatically collected when you use our Services.

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
- **User Content:** We collect the User Content you create, including scripts, character data, images, and videos.
- **Usage Information:** We collect information about your use of the Services, such as the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
- Process transactions and send you related information.
- Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
- Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only in the following circumstances:

- With your consent.
- With the third-party AI and infrastructure providers described in Section 3a below, as needed to generate the content you request.
- With third-party vendors and service providers that perform services on our behalf (for example, payment processing).
- If we believe disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, to protect and defend our rights or property, or to protect the personal safety of our users or the public.

## 3a. Third-Party AI Integrations

Virelle Studios is an AI-native content generation platform. When you use a generation feature, the relevant part of your request — such as a script prompt, reference image, or voice text — is sent to the specific third-party provider that powers that feature, which may include:

- **Pollinations** and **OpenRouter** — for script, image, and text generation.
- **ElevenLabs** — for AI voice generation, which processes any text or reference audio you submit for that feature.
- **Vast.ai** — for GPU compute used in footage rendering; your generation job (not your account credentials) runs on a rented instance for the duration of the render.

Each provider processes this data under its own privacy policy, and we send only what a given feature needs to function. If you supply your own API key for a provider (BYOK), requests made with that key are yours alone — they are never shared with, or made on behalf of, any other user.

## 3b. Mobile App Permissions

Our Android and iOS apps (Virelle Studios and Swappys) may request the following device permissions, used only for the features described:

- **Camera** — for live preview and recording when creating content.
- **Microphone** — for recording audio clips you choose to include.
- **Photos/media library** — to let you select source or reference media, and to save content you choose to export.

We do not request access to your contacts, SMS messages, call log, or precise or approximate device location.

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

## 5. Your Choices

You may update, correct, or delete your account information at any time by logging into your account. 

## 6. Data Retention

We retain account and User Content data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children and are not designed for use by anyone under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at studiosvirelle@gmail.com.
