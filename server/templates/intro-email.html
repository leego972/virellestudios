The Hybrid Production Advantage

The most powerful workflow on the platform — and currently the most widely adopted — is the hybrid production method: directors import their own live-action footage directly into the project, and the AI uses it as a ground-truth reference to generate all remaining scenes.

1

Perfect Character Consistency

The AI extracts exact face geometry, skin tone, lighting response, and physical mannerisms from your footage — anchoring every generated scene to a real human reference. Character drift becomes a non-issue.
2

Maximum Creative Control

Shoot only the scenes that demand your talent's time. Let the AI handle the rest — crowd scenes, establishing shots, action sequences, and transitions — all matched seamlessly to your footage.
3

Fraction of the Traditional Cost

A small amount of expensive talent time — extended across an entire feature by AI. Studios are achieving full-length productions at a fraction of traditional budgets without compromising on visual quality or character authenticity.
