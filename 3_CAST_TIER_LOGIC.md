# Virelle Signature Cast: Tiering & Leasing Logic

## 1. The Leasing Model Overview
The Virelle Signature Cast is a premium digital talent agency built directly into Virelle Studios. The monetization strategy is based on access tiers, allowing creators to lease digital actors for their projects. This model positions Virelle actors as faster, more consistent, and commercially safer alternatives to custom AI character generation or hiring physical talent.

## 2. Cast Access Tiers

### Standard Signature Cast
- **Access Level:** Available to mid-tier (Pro) subscribers and above.
- **Usage Rights:** Standard commercial usage (social media, indie films, small ad campaigns).
- **Features:** 
  - Full actor profile access.
  - Standard consistency anchors applied to generation.
  - 2-3 accent capabilities.
- **Example Actors:** Character actors, comedic support, youth talent.

### Premium Signature Cast
- **Access Level:** Available to high-tier (Studio/Enterprise) subscribers, or via per-project microtransactions (credits) for lower tiers.
- **Usage Rights:** Broad commercial usage (broadcast, large-scale ad campaigns, theatrical release).
- **Features:**
  - Highly polished, hyper-realistic consistency anchors.
  - Extensive accent matrices (4+ dialects).
  - Specialized performance lanes (e.g., high-glamour, intense action).
  - Priority rendering queues.
- **Example Actors:** Lead roles, specialized archetypes (e.g., The Elegant Mastermind, The Passionate Antihero).

### Flagship Stars
- **Access Level:** Exclusive to top-tier Enterprise subscribers, or requires significant per-project credit expenditure.
- **Usage Rights:** Full commercial buyout options, exclusive booking periods (the actor cannot be used by competing brands during the lease).
- **Features:**
  - Maximum fidelity consistency anchors (flawless close-ups, perfect micro-expressions).
  - Custom voice model fine-tuning for specific project needs.
  - "Publicly Recognizable" status (marketed by Virelle as standalone digital celebrities).
- **Example Actors:** The breakout stars of the Virelle original launch series.

## 3. Product Integration Logic

### The "Lease" Workflow
1. **Browse & Preview:** All users, regardless of tier, can browse the entire Signature Cast roster and view profiles.
2. **Preview Generation (Watermarked):** Lower-tier users can generate watermarked test shots using Premium or Flagship actors to check scene fit.
3. **Casting (Leasing):** 
   - If the user has the required subscription tier, they click "Cast" and the actor is immediately available in their project workspace.
   - If the user lacks the tier, they are prompted to either upgrade their subscription or spend a one-time credit fee to unlock the actor for that specific project.
4. **Commercial Licensing (Future Phase):** Enterprise users can negotiate exclusive usage rights for Flagship stars directly through a dedicated Virelle talent management portal.

## 4. Value Proposition for Creators
- **Speed:** Skip the tedious process of prompting and tuning custom characters. The Signature Cast is pre-tuned for maximum consistency.
- **Safety:** All Signature Cast members are 100% AI-generated, guaranteeing no likeness infringement issues with real-world celebrities.
- **Quality:** These actors are the highest fidelity outputs Virelle can produce, optimized for cinematic lighting and complex emotional expressions.
