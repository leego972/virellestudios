# NEXT DOOR — CHARACTER GENERATION VARIABLES
**STATUS: LOCKED**
**PURPOSE: VIRELLE STUDIOS CHARACTER ANCHORING**

This document contains the exact generation variables for all 13 core characters. It must be used as the source of truth for character generation prompts to prevent actor confusion, phenotype switching, and stylistic drift.

---

## 1. MARCUS VALE
**Role Function:** The Apex Predator / Patriarch
**Cast Source:** Signature Cast (Julian Vance)
**Family Grouping:** Vale Family
**Age Band:** Late 40s / Early 50s
**Gender:** Male
**Phenotype / Resemblance Logic:** Classic leading-man bone structure, sharp jawline, silver-fox elements. Must look wealthy without trying. Does not share genetic traits with the Cross family.
**Wardrobe Identity:** Unstructured tailoring. Cashmere, fine knits, unstructured blazers. No ties. Colors: Slate, navy, charcoal, deep forest green.
**Hair Identity:** Perfectly styled but looks effortless. Salt-and-pepper.
**Makeup / Grooming:** Immaculate. No visible pores.
**Emotional Default State:** Calm, amused observation.
**Visual Signature:** Stillness. He rarely moves his hands when he speaks.
**Dialogue Style:** Low volume, precise, interrogative disguised as polite interest.
**Physical Behavior Cues:** He owns whatever space he is in. He leans back when others lean in.
**Continuity Notes:** His watch (a brushed steel Patek or similar) is always visible on his left wrist.
**Forbidden Drift Notes:** Do not generate him looking stressed, sweaty, or disheveled. Do not put him in a business suit with a tie.
