## Section 3: Premium Casting UX — Final Spec

The Talent Search is a premium digital talent agency, not a library. Every design decision reinforces this.

**Flagship Star Positioning** is achieved through visual distinction: Flagship Stars occupy 2x2 grid cards in the roster view, hover states play a 2-second micro-expression video loop, and a gold "Seen in The Veil" watermark appears on series actors' profiles.

**Premium Unlock Logic** is non-punitive: all users can browse and preview. When a lower-tier user attempts to cast a restricted actor, the "Cast" button becomes a gold "Unlock Actor" button that leads to a value-first pitch modal before any payment screen.

**Chemistry Pair Casting** is surfaced on every actor's profile page. Pre-tested pairings are tagged with their specific dynamic (e.g., *Adversarial Romance*, *Intellectual Rivalry*). A single "Cast Both" button adds the pair to the project.

**Project-Side Cast Roster Visibility** is maintained through a persistent sidebar in the project workspace, showing cinematic headshots of all currently cast actors. Actors can be dragged directly into scene blocks.

**"Cast a Virelle Star" Branding** uses aspirational language throughout: "Cast," "Audition," "Roster," "Chemistry." The feature is always branded as the *Virelle Signature Cast*.
