# Premium Casting UX & Final Verdict

## 1. The Premium Casting UX
The Talent Search feature is not a library; it is a digital talent agency. The UX must reflect the premium nature of the actors.

### Flagship Star Positioning
The top tier of actors (the "Breakout 6") are positioned as true digital celebrities.
- **Visual Distinction:** In the grid view, Flagship Stars occupy larger, 2x2 grid cards.
- **Hover States:** Instead of a static image, hovering over a Flagship Star plays a high-resolution, 2-second video loop of a signature micro-expression (e.g., Julian Vance's smirk, Sofia Reyes's slow blink).
- **Watermarks:** Actors who starred in the launch series carry a subtle gold "Seen in The Veil" watermark on their profile, instantly communicating premium value.

### Premium Unlock Logic
The monetization model is built directly into the casting flow without feeling punitive.
- **Visibility:** All users can view all profiles and generate watermarked test shots with any actor.
- **The Upgrade Path:** When a user attempts to cast a Premium or Flagship actor without the requisite subscription tier, the standard "Cast" button is replaced by a gold "Unlock Actor" button.
- **The Pitch:** Clicking "Unlock Actor" does not immediately ask for a credit card. It opens a modal showcasing the actor's premium features: *Highest Fidelity Rendering, 4+ Accent Models, Exclusive Wardrobe Anchors.* The user is then offered a one-click subscription upgrade or a one-time project credit fee.

### Chemistry Pair Casting
A core feature designed to increase multi-actor casting (and therefore, platform lock-in).
- **The Match UI:** On every actor's profile page, a "Chemistry Matches" section highlights 2-3 pre-tested co-stars.
- **Contextual Tagging:** These pairs are not random. They are tagged with their specific dynamic (e.g., Julian Vance + Sofia Reyes = *Adversarial Romance*; Kenji Sato + Elena Rostova = *Intellectual Rivalry*).
- **Frictionless Casting:** A single "Cast Both" button allows the user to instantly add the pair to their project, knowing the AI engine has been pre-tuned to render them together perfectly.

### Project-Side Cast Roster Visibility
Once an actor is cast, their presence must be constantly reinforced.
- **The Persistent Roster:** A sleek sidebar appears on the right side of the main project workspace. It displays the cinematic headshots of the currently cast actors.
- **Drag-and-Drop Generation:** Users can drag an actor's headshot directly into a scene block or dialogue prompt to automatically apply their consistency anchors and voice model.

### "Cast a Virelle Star" Branding
The language of the platform elevates the product.
- We do not use terms like "Select Model," "Library," or "Asset."
- We use "Cast," "Audition," "Roster," and "Chemistry."
- The feature is consistently branded as the *Virelle Signature Cast*.
